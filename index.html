FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does StockBridge sync inventory?
StockBridge uses Shopify webhooks to detect inventory changes in real-time. When a product sells or is restocked on one store, the webhook fires and StockBridge updates all connected stores within seconds.
Do both stores need to pay?
No. Only the source (primary) store pays for the plan. Destination stores install StockBridge completely free and inherit all features from the source store's plan.
How are products matched between stores?
StockBridge auto-detects matching products by scanning barcodes and SKUs across both stores. Products with matching barcodes or exact SKU matches are automatically paired. You can also manually map products.
What happens if both stores sell at the same time?
StockBridge includes echo prevention to avoid infinite sync loops. When a sync update is made by StockBridge, it recognizes the change and prevents re-syncing it back.
Can I sync more than 2 stores?
Yes. Growth supports 5 stores, Business supports 10, and Enterprise has unlimited stores. All connected stores sync with each other automatically.
Is there a free plan?
Yes. The Free plan includes 50 SKUs, 2 stores, and one-way sync. It is available forever with no credit card required.
